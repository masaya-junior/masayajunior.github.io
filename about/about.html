I’m a producer, multi-instrumentalist, occasional vocalist, and DJ, who makes music in different capacities, be they film score contributions, podcast theme music, engineering and mixing for friends, or composing as a singer-songwriter. The Masaya Junior alias is mainly a beatmaking and production-oriented project, which aims to combine the electronic, club, and soul influences which have defined the past 5-10 years of my musical life with the guitar-based dream pop, shoegaze, and ambient music that influenced me when I first started recording music. I'm lucky enough to have grown up in 6 countries on 4 different continents, which offers a worldview and a perticular sense of curiosity and wonder that I try to incorporate into every creative project, particularly this one.
